Glider

The glider workshop introduces students to the principles of flight and aerodynamics. Each team will design, build, and test their glider prototypes, gaining valuable insights into the world of aviation.

Duration: 2 days
Cost per student: Rs. 300 per student (2 per team)
Venue: Auditorium
Requirement: Well-ventilated indoor space for glider testing
